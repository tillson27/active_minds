Wellness Retreat for School Professionals

Preparing for the School Year with Compassion, Clarity, and Sustainability

A full-day experiential retreat to help educators and school-based professionals reconnect with wellbeing, passion, and purpose — before the new school year begins.

You care for so many. This day is for you. Practical tools. Deep reflection. Meaningful connection.
This retreat will help you
  • Develop grounding and nervous-system regulation strategies
  • Increase awareness of stress patterns and protective responses
  • Strengthen self-compassion and boundary awareness
  • Reconnect with your values and professional purpose
  • Create an individualized wellness plan for the school year
